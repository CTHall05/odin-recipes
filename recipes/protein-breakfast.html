Protein Breakfast

Cottage Cheese and Eggs on Toast

Ingredients

  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 100g of Cottage Cheese
  • 2 Slices of Seaded Brown Granary Bread
  • Some light butter
  • Teaspoon of Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Step 1: Pour your olive oil into a pan.
  2. Step 2: Break your eggs into the pan on a medium heat.
  3. Step 3: Place your two pieces of bread into the toaster for 2 to 3 minutes.
  4. Step 4: During this time check on your eggs and generously add salt an pepper to the eggs.
  5. Step 5: Once the eggs are cooked to your preferance, turn off the heat your toast should be ready.
  6. Step 6: Once the toast is done, serve on a plate spreading butter on the toast and then equally spread your cottage cheese on top.
  7. Step 7: Finally add your eggs to the plate, salt and pepper over the toast to finish off.

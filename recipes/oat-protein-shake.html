Protein Oat Shake

CProtein Oat Shake

Ingredients

  • 100g of Oats
  • 2 Scoops of Protein Power of Choice
  • 30g of 100% Peanut Butter
  • 400ml of water or milk of choice

Steps

  1. Step 1: Pour all ingredients into your blender.
  2. Step 2: Blend for 10 secconds or until thick.
  3. Step 3: Pour out protein shake and enjoy!

Farmers Breakfast

Ingredients

  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 500g of Potatoes
  • 1 Red Onion
  • 2 Teaspoons of Olive Oil
  • 50g of Mozzerella
  • 50g of Spinache

Steps

  1. Step 1: Pour your olive oil into a pan.
  2. Step 2: Finely slice your potatoes into thin circles
  3. Step 3: Place your finely sliced potatoes into the pan on a medium heat for 10minutes, stir every 3 minuets.
  4. Step 4: During this time finely dice your onion.
  5. Step 5: After 10 minuets place the onion into the pan to join the potatoes.
  6. Step 6: .Fry for a further 10 minuets sirring again every 3 minuets.
  7. Step 7: During this time finely chop your spinache and mozzerela, whisk your two eggs.
  8. Step 8: Once the ten minuets are up throw in your egss, mozzerlla and spinache and give it a quick stir before waiting a futher 2 minuets.
